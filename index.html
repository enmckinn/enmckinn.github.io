About Me

I'm Erin. I am working on a Graphic Information Technology degree from Arizona State University and have recently completed the #VetsWhoCode bootcamp, all with the intention of becoming a web designer and developer. My employment background includes working for the government, for profit and not-for-profit companies. The job roles have primarily been in customer support roles. I am looking forward to transitioning into the tech industry.

My interests lie in the user experience and accessability realm of web design and development. I think the web can be functional and look good, as well as be easy to navigate and interact with, for everyone.

Right now, I am looking to find a fit with a company as a web designer that will allow me to create meaningful design as well as work on improving my development skills.
