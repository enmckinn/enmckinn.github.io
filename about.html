My Background

I started out my adult working life in the Army after graduating from college. I started in the Army as an Ordinance Officer and decided to become an Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD) Officer during my Officer Basic Course. After completing training I took my first position as a small group leader. After being in that position for a while, I was moved to an assistant operations position, helping to plan larger level operations. My last assignment as an active duty Soldier was to remain stateside while the unit deployed to the theater of operations and provide the support needed from the States. After completing my Active Duty requirements, I transitioned into the NC Army National Guard where I acted as an Ammuition Officer.

My civilian experience started out in a car dealership as a cashier in the service department. I did that until a position became available as a receptionist and customer service associate in the accounting department. Once in the accounting department, I worked my way to being responsilbe for all digital aspects of the dealership to include the website, social media and reputation management.
